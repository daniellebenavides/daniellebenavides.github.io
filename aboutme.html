About Me

Howdy! I am a graduate of the Texas A & M University Class of 2020. I graduated Magna Cum Laude, with a bachelor in Environmental Design and a Minor in Art in the College of Architecture. I am currently pursuing my Master's in Science of Visualization at Texas A & M University with a strong focus on 3D Modeling, Look Development, Surfacing, and Texturing, as well as Concept Art, Character Design, and Storyboarding.


Much of my dedication has always been towards my studies and striving to improve my creativity and artistic nature. I am a person who desires to bring life into creative pieces and seeks to inspire others as many have come to inspire me. I constantly seek ways to improve myself and build up my confidence. Thus, this portfolio is full of creative pieces, academic and personal, that I hope to continue to build through my professional career.


I am constantly seeking personal improvement to better my knowledge in the creative realm and build my artistic style. I seek to work with others to reach a common goal, building friendships, and connections with each person I meet. Moving forward, I am eager to assist in bringing life to the creative realm of digital media and design; offering up my skills and leaving a lasting mark on others.
